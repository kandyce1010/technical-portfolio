description: Phase 2 AI content enhancement for workshop participants. Offers prompts to improve project descriptions, about page, blog ideas, and contact copy.
---

Help the user enhance their portfolio content with AI. This is Phase 2 of the workshop — the user already has a working 3-page portfolio from Phase 1.

Ask which area they want to improve first:
1. Project descriptions
2. Professional summary (About page)
3. Blog post ideas
4. About page narrative
5. Contact page copy

Then use the appropriate prompt template below.

---

### 1. Improve a project description

Ask the user to paste their current description. Then rewrite it to:
- Highlight the technical challenge solved
- Be compelling to recruiters and technical peers
- Stay at 2-3 sentences, professional tone

---

### 2. Generate a professional summary

Ask for:
- Career highlights (bullet points fine)
- Key technical skills
- What they're looking for or focused on

Write a 2-3 sentence summary that leads with their unique value and sounds like a person, not a resume.

---

### 3. Blog post ideas

Ask for their technical background and focus area. Suggest 3 post topics that would:
- Showcase real expertise
- Be genuinely useful to other developers
- Be specific enough to actually write

For each: catchy title, 2-sentence description, why it matters.

---

### 4. Enhance About page narrative

Ask the user to paste a career timeline entry. Rewrite it to be more narrative — tell the growth story, not just the job facts.

---

### 5. Polish Contact page copy

Ask for:
- What they want to be contacted about
- Their communication style (professional, friendly, direct, etc.)

Write a 2-3 sentence intro that's warm and encourages people to reach out.

---

## Tips to share with the user

- Always edit AI output to match your own voice
- Fact-check any technical claims the AI adds
- If the result is too verbose, ask to trim to 2-3 sentences
- Your authentic experience is the content — AI just polishes the presentation
